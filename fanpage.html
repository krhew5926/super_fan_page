Background Information

Basketball was created in 1891 by a man named James Naismith, who made the game originally with only 13 rules. He played using two soccer balls and two peach baskets, and it's original purpose was to condition athletes during colder weather. The game has developed so much since then and it is now the 2nd most watched sport in the United States, behind American Football.

My favorite shot in basketball is the three-point shot, and the skill I am best at is dribbling the ball.
